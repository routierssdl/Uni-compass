Introduction

Welcome to our website showcasing universities in Lebanon. Here you can explore a comprehensive list of Lebanese universities along with details about their faculties and majors.

How to Navigate

- Use the side navigation panel on the left (or click the burger icon at the top left to collapse/expand it).
- Click "Home" to return to this page.
- Click "Universities" to view a list of universities. From there, click on any university to see detailed information about its faculties and majors.
